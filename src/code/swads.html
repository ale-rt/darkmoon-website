Vista vs Linux

Per me una distribuzione vale l'altra: ritengo sia solo questione di gusto personale. Personalmente uso da anni e con soddisfazione Mandriva Linux perche' mi trovo bene con urpmi, il suo gestore dei pacchetti, e si trovano gia' compilati molti software che uso con gusto!

Come ambiente di lavoro sono abituato ad usare KDE e mi trovo veramente a mio agio!

Vista vs Linux

For me a distribution is equivalent to the others: it is only matter of personal tastes. I tried a lot of them (Fedora, *Ubuntu, Suse, Debian, Slackware, Gentoo, ...) but at the end I always come back! :) I am curious about Sabayon linux, an Italian distribution based on gentoo: I have to try it when I have some free time! I use Mandriva with satisfaction since years Mandriva Linux: I feel very comfortable with its package manager, urpmi, and I can easily install and mantain up to date almost all the software I need!

My Desktop Environment choice is KDE and I am so used to it that I can hardly replace it, even if I like from time to time to try something else!
